Master's Thesis

"Procedural Narrative Generation in Open World Games"

Abstract

This research explores how procedural generation can create meaningful narratives in open world games without sacrificing player agency. Through the development of a prototype game, "Tales of the Shifting Isles," I demonstrate a system that generates quests, character dialogues, and world events based on player actions and choices.

About Me

Filipe Silveira

Game Designer & Developer

I'm a game designer passionate about creating experiences that blend meaningful narratives with innovative mechanics. With a Master's degree in Game Design and 5+ years of experience, I've worked on projects ranging from small experimental prototypes to larger narrative-driven games.

My approach to game design focuses on player agency and emergent storytelling. I believe the best games are those where players feel their choices truly matter, creating unique stories they'll want to share.
